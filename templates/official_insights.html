{{ _('Ergot Disease Survey in Pearl Millet - Marathwada Region') }}

{{ _('A comprehensive survey across nine tehsils in Chh. Sambhaji Nagar district (Kharif 2023-24) assessed ergot disease prevalence. Disease incidence ranged from 5.50%% (Phulambari) to 22.00%% (Kannad), with a mean of 13.19%%.
